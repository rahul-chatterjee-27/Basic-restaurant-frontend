About Us

Where Passion Meets Flavor!

Our Story

Welcome to Velvet Fork, where passion meets flavor! We offer a diverse menu of delicious dishes, crafted with fresh ingredients and authentic recipes. From hearty breakfasts to comforting dinners, refreshing drinks, and delightful desserts, there’s something for everyone. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and exceptional taste ensures a memorable dining experience. Explore our menu, filter your favorites, and indulge in the best of food and hospitality. Great taste, great moments—only at Velvet Fork!

Our Journey

2015 - The Beginning

Started as a small family-run eatery with a vision to serve fresh, flavorful dishes.

2018 - Growing Popularity

Launched an online ordering platform, making our food accessible anywhere.

2020 - Digital Expansion

Expanded to a second location due to overwhelming customer love and demand.

2023 - Culinary Recognition

Awarded ‘Best Fine Dining Experience’ in the city and featured in food magazines.

2025 - Sustainable and Future Ready

Committed to sustainability, farm-to-table ingredients, and innovative dining experiences.
